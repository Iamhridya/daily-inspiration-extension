# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Daily Inspiration respects your privacy.

## Information Collected

This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

## Local Storage

Favorite quotes may be stored locally in your browser using Chrome storage APIs.

The extension may request motivational quotes from public quote APIs.

No personally identifiable information is sent.
